About

A largely self-taught developer, I grew up using the command line, writing BASIC programs, and creating simple websites for the Web 1.0. While studying at the University of Washington, I learned more complex programming concepts through the DXARTS program, using Common LISP and SuperCollider to compose music. Since then, I've honed my web development skills through various courses, community projects, and my employment and freelance work.
